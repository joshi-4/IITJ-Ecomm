About us

Hey Guys this is the IIT Jodhpur E-Commerce site. This is an OLX type of website. You can use it to buy and sell stuff from/to other students.

To Buy:

  1. Goto Buy in navbar.
  2. Click on view-item on the item you want to buy.
  3. Contact the seller.

To Sell:

  1. To sell you need to be logged in.
  2. If you are not an existing user then Register else directly LogIn.
  3. Click on the Sell button on navbar.
  4. Add the item.

To Remove a Sold item:

  1. You need to be Logged In.
  2. Goto to Profile. It shows your existing items.
  3. Click on the Remove button on item you want to remove.
